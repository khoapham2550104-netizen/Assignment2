2Introduction
In the previous part, we followed the Straw Hat Pirates on their journey at Water 7, where internal conflicts gradually surfaced and critical decisions were made. The climax of these events occurred when Nico Robin was captured by the World Government and taken to Enies Lobby—one of the three most powerful judicial institutions of the Government.
At Enies Lobby, the Straw Hat Pirates are forced into a direct confrontation with the elite CP9 agents in order to rescue their crewmate. This battle is not merely a clash between individuals, but also a struggle against time, the battlefield environment, and various tactical factors. Structures such as the Main Gate, the Courthouse, the Tower of Justice, and the Bridge of Hesitation play crucial roles, directly influencing the progress of the rescue mission.
In this assignment, students are required to simulate the battle at Enies Lobby by designing classes and implementing interaction mechanisms among characters, buildings, and the battle state. Characters will act in turns, performing attacks or using special skills, while buildings continuously affect the overall progression of the battle.

3Classes in the Program
This assignment applies Object-Oriented Programming to model the battle in which the Straw Hat Pirates attempt to rescue Nico Robin at Enies Lobby. The entities in this assignment are represented as classes and are described as follows.

Note:

•Students are required to carefully read the problem statement to determine the inheritance relationships (derived classes), as these will not be explicitly specified.
•Students are allowed to define additional attributes, methods, or classes as needed to support the implementation of the required classes in this assignment.
•Within the scope of this assignment, whenever computed values need to be rounded to integers, students must round up.
•Within the scope of this assignment, the values of hp, energy, morale, alarmLevel, rescueProgress, escapeProgress, and the hp of buildings must always be clamped to their valid ranges after each update. Specifically, a character’s hp must lie within the range from 0 to maxHp, a building’s hp must lie within the range from 0 to maxHP, and energy, morale, alarmLevel, rescueProgress, and escapeProgress must lie within the range from 0 to 100. In addition, busterCallTimer must not be less than 0.

Battle Rules:

1.Target selection rules for the Straw Hat Pirates:
•If MainGate is not destroyed, the target is MainGate.
•If MainGate is destroyed, alarmLevel >= 50, and Courthouse is not destroyed, the target is Courthouse.
•If the above conditions are not satisfied and busterCallTimer <= 5 and BusterCallShip is not destroyed, the target is BusterCallShip.
•If none of the above conditions are satisfied and Robin has not been rescued, the target is the first alive CP9 agent in the cp9Agents array.
•If Robin has been rescued, the target is BridgeOfHesitation. In this case, the character’s action is considered as supporting the escape; if this building is destroyed or does not exist, the character attacks the first alive CP9 agent.

•If the current character is Chopper and has enough energy to use specialSkill, the target is the Straw Hat member with the lowest hp.
2.Target selection rules for CP9: during the turn of a CP9 agent, the target is always the first alive Straw Hat member in the strawHats array. CP9 agents do not attack buildings.
3.Skill usage rules:
•During a character’s turn, if the character has enough energy to use specialSkill, it will use specialSkill. Otherwise, it will use attack.
•The energy cost of each skill is specified in the corresponding character class. After using a skill, the character’s energy must decrease by the corresponding cost. If there is not enough energy, the skill cannot be executed.
4.Character attack rules:
•When the target is a character, the damage produced by attack or specialSkill is applied to the target. If the target’s health becomes less than or equal to 0, it is set to 0 and the target is considered defeated.
•If the attacker belongs to the Straw Hat Pirates and defeats the target, morale in-creases by 5. If the attacker belongs to CP9 and defeats the target, morale decreases by 5.
